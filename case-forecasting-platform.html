Work / Forecasting Platform

Turning raw distributor data into forecasts teams actually trust

Add a screenshot of the forecasting dashboard here

Challenge

Distributors were forecasting demand across spreadsheets and gut feeling, with every region reading the same numbers differently. There was no shared source of truth, so overstock and stockouts kept happening in the same breath — and nobody could say which forecast to trust.

Approach

I started by mapping every data source feeding into the forecast and sitting with sales and ops teams to understand how they actually made decisions day to day. From there, I stripped the dashboard down to the handful of metrics that mattered, replaced dense tables with scannable trend views, and introduced a confidence indicator so teams could see how reliable a given forecast was before acting on it. We iterated the prototype with real distributor data across three rounds of feedback before the first release.
