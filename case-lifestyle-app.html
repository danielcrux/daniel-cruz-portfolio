Work / Lifestyle App

A daily companion app designed around real habits, not streak guilt

Add a screenshot of the app here

Challenge

Most habit apps punish a single missed day with a broken streak, which is exactly when people give up and delete the app. The goal was a companion that tracked real habits and mood without the guilt spiral — something people would still open on their worst week, not just their best one.

Approach

I reframed the core loop around a weekly rhythm instead of daily streaks, so one off day never resets your progress. Onboarding was cut down to three screens, the visual language stayed calm and low-pressure, and I rewrote notification copy to sound encouraging rather than punitive. We tested the flow with a small beta group and adjusted the tone and pacing based on how they actually used it, not how we assumed they would.
