Work / Checkout & Pickup

Making in-store pickup as fast and painless as checkout should be

Add a screenshot of the pickup flow here

Challenge

The pickup experience felt bolted onto checkout instead of designed alongside it. Order status was vague, customers weren't sure when to head to the store, and staff had no easy way to tell who had actually arrived — which meant long waits at the counter for something that should've taken seconds.

Approach

I mapped the full journey from adding an item to cart all the way to walking out with it in hand, and rebuilt the status states around what the customer needed to know at each step. Real-time "ready for pickup" notifications replaced generic order updates, and I designed a simple companion view for store staff to see who was arriving and when. We piloted the flow in two stores before rolling it out further.
